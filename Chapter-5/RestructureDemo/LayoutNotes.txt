General size class to device mapping

wCompact hCompact:  
All iPhones except 6+ in landscape

wCompact hRegular:
All iPhones in portrait.

wRegular hCompact:
iPhone 6 Plus in landscape.

wRegular hRegular:
All iPads in both landscape and portrait.
